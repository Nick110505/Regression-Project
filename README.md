# Regression-Project
Full regression project where the goal was to figure out which variables were best to use and to make a regression model using those variables
Here is the research question:

Jake wants to open a baby store chain with the hope of expanding his business overseas. He is interested
in finding out whether the average sales are associated with the characteristics of the store location. To
understand this relationship, he gathered data on car seat sales for 500 locations. Attributes include:
Sales: Sales (in thousands of dollars) at each location
Price: Price company charges for car seats at each site
CompPrice: Price charged by competitor at each location
Income: Median income in the community (in 1000’s of dollars)
Education: Education level at the location
Age: Average age of the local population
Advertising: Local advertising budget for company at each location (in thousands of dollars)
Accidents: Average monthly accidents in the area
Urban: A factor with levels: 0 and 1 to indicate whether the store is in an urban or rural location
(1 if urban 0 if rural)
US: A factor with levels 0 and 1 to indicate whether the store is in the US or not (1 if in US 0 if
other)
ShelveLoc: A factor with levels Bad, Good and Medium indicating the quality of the shelving
location for the car seats at each site
Logan is seeking your assistance to build a model to make inferences and predictions about the car seat sales.
The research questions to be answered in your project are:
1. Are any of the features measured, useful in predicting the car seat sales at a given location in the target
population? If so, what are they and how do they affect the the car seat sales?
2. How can the measured features be used to predict the car seat sales at a given location?
